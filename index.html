Developing Custom Solutions Tailored to Your Needs.

With over 20 years of experience in front-end development, UX/UI design, and full-stack engineering, GDS Consulting specialises in creating accessible, inclusive, and high-performing digital experiences. As accessibility advocates and contributors to the W3C, our work focuses on ensuring compliance with WCAG, EAA, AUIG, and ADA standards while optimizing usability for neurodivergent users. Whether its building scalable UI frameworks, conducting in-depth accessibility audits, or enhancing brand identity with accessible theming, We provide end-to-end solutions tailored to your needs.
